Hello, I’m Alexandra
Digital Designer based in Orlando who specializes in UX & UI design.

view resume

I'm a self-taught front-end developer turned digital designer. My job as a designer is to be the voice of the user. To build user interfaces that are functional, accessible, and visually appealing. I enjoy turning complex problems into simple, beautiful and intuitive interface designs.

What I love most about UX is engaging in creativity and problem solving, while making an impact in my community. When I'm not pushing pixels or coding, I enjoy watching Korean dramas, thrifting, finding a new eatery, or hanging out with my pups.

If you're insterested, let's work on a project together!
