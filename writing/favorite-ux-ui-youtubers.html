It can be overwhelming searching the internet for tutorials when you’re first starting out in your path to design. I have created a list of my favorite Youtuber’s UX & UI channels so that you can stop stressing and start designing

These channels are not in any order, they’re all equally great!

UX youtube channels

the futur

This channel is about the business of design and has great interviews with industry professionals. They have a great UX/UI playlist that you can find here but here's the first video of the playlist

sarah doody

Sarah Doody is a UX designer and consultant who shares her UX expertise with us. Her channel is great to watch if you’re preparing for an interview.

flux

Ran Segall’s channel gives brief advice on many topics such as his favorite design tools, his design journey or just tips if you’re just starting out in design.

mike locke

Mike Locke is one of the first Youtuber’s I started following when I first started learning about UX. He has great resources if you’re getting ready for a UX interview but also if you want to know more about UX, UI and their differences. He also offers a lot of motivational videos if you need to feel inspired.

laith wallace

Laith Wallace’s channel is great for UX tips and also to learn about various aspects of UX such as how to get started in UX, the design thinking process, and his own experience in the field of UX.

UI youtube channels

caler edwards

If you’re a fan of the Adobe XD, Caler Edwards is your guy! You will learn so many techniques while staying up-to-date with the latest Adobe XD tips & tricks.

sketch together

I don’t know what I like most about Pablo Stanley’s channel - his tutorials or his personality. As the name says, he makes tutorials specifically for Sketch but also has tutorials using other design tools such as Photoshop, Principle and Webflow. You can find his full Sketch App crash course here.

maex

If you’re looking to learn more about design using Sketch, Maex is your guy! He has some great Sketch tutorials but he doesn’t just stop there. He also has Principle, XD, Webflow and many other tutorials. I suggest checking out all his videos.

matej novak

He hasn’t updated his channel in over a year but you can learn a lot about Adobe XD through his speed art videos.

XO pixel

I followed her when I started learning about HTML and CSS but she has great XD and Photoshop videos. If you’re looking into getting into web design, she also has tutorials on those.

other notable design-related channels

mackenzie child

Mackenzie is an amazing illustrator. A lot of his videos are follow alongs but he also has some Illustrator tutorials if you’re just getting started. You can find his Adobe Illustrator essentials playlist here.

gigantic

If you want to learn how to design characters for your next design project, check out this channel. He focuses more on flat character design but they are incredible.

the simple designers

Another graphic design channel that in my opinion shows a lot of modern design techniques. There’s not a lot of videos so I suggest browsing through them and see what catches your eyes.
